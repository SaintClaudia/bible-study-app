Supporting the
journey of initiation

Designed to help those new to the faith, Bible Study makes it easy for participants to explore the life of Christ, learn the foundations of the Catholic faith, and deepen their relationship with God.

Participants can use Bible Study to:

  • Prepare for Sunday Mass with weekly readings and reflection
  • Learn the foundations of the faith through short, easy-to-follow lessons
  • Find trusted answers to common questions about the faith
  • Discover recommended books and other trusted Catholic resources

If you believe this could benefit your participants, we'd be grateful if you shared it with your OCIA class.
